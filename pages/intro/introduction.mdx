## Welcome to my 'What I Learned' project!

As a recent graduate and aspiring software developer, I understand that the
learning never ends. I'm constantly picking up new skills and
knowledge, and I wanted to find a way to keep track of everything
I'm learning as I progress in my career.

## What is What I Learned?

This project is my personal coding notebook where I document my
journey as a fresher graduate and share the lessons I learn along
the way. It's a space where I can reflect on my accomplishments,
note down new concepts and ideas, and track my progress as I build
my skills. Whether it's a new programming language, a new tool or
technique, or a new workflow, this project helps me stay organized
and on top of my learning. I'm excited to see how this project will
grow and evolve as I continue to develop my skills and knowledge as
a software developer.
